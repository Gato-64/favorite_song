El Gato Que Está Triste Y Azul (Un Gato Nel Blu)

By Roberto Carlos

The song "El Gato Que Esta Triste y Azul", also referred to as "Un Gato en la Oscuridad" due to repeated translations, translates in english to "The Cat That is Sad and Blue" or "A Cat in the Dark".The song is performed by Brazilan singer-songwriter Roberto Carlos and was written by Italian composers Gaetano "Totò" Savio and Giancarlo Bigazzi.

Released in 1972 under the CBS label, the studio album went on to become one of Billboard Magazines' 50 Greatest Latin Albums of the Last 50 Years in 2015. The song potrays a melancholc cat longing for a loved one who has been lost, and the loneliness and yearning that accompanies the emotions of love in youth and loss.
